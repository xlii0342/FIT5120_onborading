Effects of UV Radiation on Skin

UV radiation from the sun can damage skin and eyes. UVA causes aging, while UVB causes sunburn.

  • Short-term: Sunburn, redness, pain
  • Long-term: Premature aging, wrinkles, skin cancer risk

Sun Protection Methods

Sunscreen

  • SPF: 30+ recommended (blocks 97% UVB)
  • Type: Choose broad spectrum (UVA+UVB protection)
  • Apply: 15-30 minutes before going outside
  • Reapply: Every 2 hours and after swimming

Clothing

  • UPF: Look for UPF 50+ fabrics
  • Coverage: Long sleeves, pants when possible
  • Hat: Wide-brimmed (3+ inches)
  • Sunglasses: 100% UV protection/UV400

Timing & Shade

  • Avoid: 10 AM - 4 PM (peak UV hours)
  • Shade: Seek trees, umbrellas, structures
  • Shadow rule: Short shadow = high UV
  • Check: Daily UV index forecasts
